Seek Refuge



Seek Refuge is a radio show I present and broadcast on RTM FM monthly. it has a focus on contemporary classical / composed music and dives into other experimental music & sound of various sorts. Each show has some sort of theme, sometimes vague (like... a mood) and sometimes more focussed on a composer, instrument or period etc.
It is a bit of fun! and helps me to discover new stuff all the time, I also rlly enjoy the curation process . Here are a few recent showss which you can listen back to.
